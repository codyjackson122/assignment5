Roster

The Minnesota Twins have constructed a team for high preformance and with a winning spirit in the clubhouse, here are the three big contributors

Byron Buxton

Carlos Correa

Joe Ryan

Byron Buxton, you know and love him, and he signed a new massive seven year deal with a new trade clause, so dont expect him to leave anytime soon. With blazing speed and unbelievable power, he is a true five tool player.

Carlos Correa, off of ten year run with the Houston Astros, Joins the twins on a three deal for the highest paid middle infielder in history. He is a former platinum glove and gold glove winner with some pop in his bat

Joe Ryan is a rookied traded in the 2020 season for designated hitter Nelson Cruz. This young rookie is looking to make a splash, and has so far shown the capabilities of a young ace for this brand new rotation
